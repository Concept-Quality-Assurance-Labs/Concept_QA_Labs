While upgrading CMMI from v1.1 to v1.2 in August 2006, Software Engineering Institute (SEI®), the governor of CMMI®, made the interpretation of High Maturity Process Areas very clear. Without strong Metrics foundation and Process Performance Management, moving to higher maturity is not possible. Our product “PPB -Process Performance Manager” helps organizations in laying out a strong Metrics and Process Performance foundation.

Some of the powerful features offered by PPM include

* Setting up of the measurement program by choosing applicable metrics from list of various metrics offered by the tool

* Quick setup and Ease of Use

* Statistical Process Control of various parameters

* Cost of Quality (COQ) Management

* Auto escalation in case of high variances

* Power packed features for Defect Prevention & Causal Analysis

* Automated Measurement Repository

* Collaborate with time sheets management systems

* Seamless integration with Microsoft Excel e.g. Import/Export metrics data

* Automated Dashboards

* Facilitation of Analysis of Metrics through various Quality Tools

* Automated Process Performance Baselines

* Facilitate generation of Process Performance Models
